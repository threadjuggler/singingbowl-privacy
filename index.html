Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SingingBowl ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data practices associated with our App.

1. Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All app settings and user-created content (meditation schemes) are stored locally on your device only.

However, the App integrates third-party services that may collect certain data:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

If you have not purchased the "Remove Ads" option, the App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data (country/region level)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)

If you make an in-app purchase (e.g. "Remove Ads"), the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment information. Google may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The third-party data collection described above serves the following purposes:

  • Advertising — to display relevant ads within the App (AdMob)
  • Purchase processing — to handle in-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

3. Consent for Personalized Ads (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the App will request your consent before showing personalized advertisements, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You may choose to:

  • Consent to personalized ads based on your interests
  • Decline personalized ads — you will still see ads, but they will not be tailored to your interests

You can change your consent choice at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings, which will trigger a new consent prompt.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with the following third parties solely for the purposes described above:

  • Google LLC — for advertising (AdMob) and in-app purchase processing (Google Play Billing)

5. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Data collected by third-party services (Google) is subject to their respective retention policies.

App settings and meditation schemes stored locally on your device are retained until you uninstall the App or clear its data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of access — request information about what data is processed
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction — request restriction of data processing
  • Right to object — object to data processing
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw your consent for personalized ads at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with a supervisory authority in your member state

To exercise any of these rights regarding data collected by Google, please refer to Google's Privacy Controls. To exercise rights regarding this App, contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
